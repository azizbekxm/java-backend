#### Recommended level: 
- Junior / Middle
#### Required time for the course: 
* Theory: ~7h mandatory (3h optional)
* Practise: ~4h mandatory (2h optional) 

### Topics:
- JUnit 5
- Mocking and spying
- TDD and BDD approaches
- Testing best practices
