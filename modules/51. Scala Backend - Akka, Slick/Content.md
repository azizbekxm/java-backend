3. [Akka Stream](https://doc.akka.io/docs/akka/current/stream/index.html)
4. [circe](https://circe.github.io/circe/)
5. [pureconfig](https://pureconfig.github.io/)
6. [slick](https://scala-slick.org/)

**Required time for the course:** 
=======================
- Tasks: ~10h mandatory
